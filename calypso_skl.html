Calypso SKL • Autonomous Underwater Vehicle

The Autonomous Underwater Vehicle (AUV) is our signature robot. It has been designed to be able to travel underwater without human control i.e. Level 4 Autonomy and achieve tasks like Coral Exploration, Surveillance and Defence, Marine Life Monitoring etc. We at dreadnought work on systems and tech that would help us make our AUV better, trying to widen our scope of research and application.
