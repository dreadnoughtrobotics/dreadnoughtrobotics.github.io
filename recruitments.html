A special team at VIT is a unique opportunity for students to pursue their passion for a particular subject while learning and growing in a team environment. These teams are entirely student-funded and formed by a group of dedicated individuals who are eager to explore their field of interest. By joining a special team, students can participate in inter-college, national, and even international competitions, allowing them to showcase their skills and compete with like-minded individuals.

VIT has 12 special teams, which adds to the exclusivity of being part of one. Being part of a special team is an enriching experience that provides students with a chance to immerse themselves in a community of like-minded individuals who are equally passionate about the same subject. Each team has a specific area of focus and provides students with opportunities to develop a range of skills, including leadership, teamwork, communication, and problem-solving.

Furthermore, being part of a special team also provides a unique perspective on the subject that the team specializes in, allowing students to gain a deeper understanding and appreciation for it. Students are allowed to join only one special team, which ensures that they can dedicate their full attention and energy towards the team's goals and objectives.

To ensure that freshers and 2nd-year students can experience and contribute to the vibrant extracurricular culture at VIT, we have decided to open special team applications to these students ONLY. Freshers and 2nd years are still in the early stages of their college journey and have a lot to learn and experience. By giving them the chance to participate in a special team, we hope to facilitate their growth and development both academically and personally.

Also, to ensure that each student can dedicate their full attention and energy towards their chosen special team, no student is allowed to be part of multiple special teams.

Additionally, if a member decides to quit a team for whatever reason, he/she will not be permitted to be a part of ANY other special team for the remainder of their academic years at VIT Chennai.

We are a special team that specializes in the field of robotics. For more information, check out: https://linktr.ee/teamdreadnoughtrobotics

Dr. Karunamurthy K, Head of School of Mechanical Engineering.

Our primary objective is to participate in the renowned Singapore Autonomous Underwater Vehicle Challenge (SAUVC). The SAUVC is an international competition held in Singapore, where we aim to develop an Autonomous Underwater Vehicle (AUV) capable of accomplishing challenging tasks within a stipulated time frame. Our team is dedicated to designing and building an AUV that can navigate through underwater obstacles, accurately identify, and locate targets, and perform a range of complex operations. In addition to the SAUVC, we also actively participate in various inter-college competitions, including the prestigious IIT Bombay Techfest.

Moreover, we are excited to announce that our team has been selected to participate in the TAC AUV Challenge scheduled to be held Norway in June 2023. The TAC AUV Challenge is a highly competitive event that brings together the best AUV teams from across the globe to compete in a series of challenging underwater tasks. Our team is geared up to take on this challenge and make a mark in the world of autonomous underwater robotics.
