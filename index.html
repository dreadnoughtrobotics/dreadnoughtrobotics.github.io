About US

About DNT

DREADNOUGHT ROBOTICS is a budding team of 58 students from VIT University, Chennai. Armed with a profound interest in the field of robotics and an unfaltering willingness to learn and explore, Dreadnought invites you onward to a journey of experimentation and discovery.

We, the members of Dreadnought Robotics, are at the helm of expanding our foundations, putting to work theoretical principles and hands-on coding skills with good engineering to produce the choicest of robots. Teamed with our bots, we zealously represent our college at various events all over the country, and in turn, use insights from these competitions to fine-tune our efforts.


About VIT

VIT is ranked no.1 Private Engineering Institute by MHRD, Govt. of India. VIT was founded in the year 1984 by the visionary Dr. G. Viswanathan .Momentarily VIT has witnessed a meteoric rise and has set an ambitious growth target. Its towering presence in technical and management education, infrastructure that meets global standards, digitalization and scholastic faculty who are a cynosure in the academic realm manifests it.VIT is the fortress of learning in technical and management education with a profusion of books ,national and international journals and magazines in the central library.

RANKING AND ACCREDITATION: Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) is ranked within the top 450 Universities in the World. It is the first institute of India to receive QS 4-Star rating in overall category and QS 5-Star rating in teaching, employability, facilities, innovation and inclusiveness. VIT is accredited by NAAC, Govt. of India with A Grade. FICCI, Industry Consortium of India, awarded VIT as “the University of the Year 2016” and "Excellence in faculty for the year 2017" in India. Govt. of India has recognized VIT, Vellore as an Institution of Eminence.
