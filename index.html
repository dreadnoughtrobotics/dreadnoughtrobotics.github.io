About US

About DNT

DREADNOUGHT ROBOTICS is a budding team of 56 students from VIT University, Chennai. Armed with a profound interest in the field of robotics and an unfaltering willingness to learn and explore, Dreadnought invites you onward to a journey of experimentation and discovery.

We, the members of Dreadnought Robotics, are at the helm of expanding our foundations, putting to work theoretical principles and hands-on coding skills with good engineering to produce the choicest of robots. Teamed with our bots, we zealously represent our college at various events all over the country, and in turn, use insights from these competitions to fine-tune our efforts.


About VIT

Vellore Institute of Technology was founded in the year 1984, and its track record of more than three decades is proof of the success of our founder, Dr. G. Vishwanathan, as an exceptional academician. The ascent of VIT up the education ladder can be attributed not just to the exceptional infrastructure they provide, but also to the innovative teaching practices they have been implementing.

In recent years, VIT has been the go-to brand for engineering, business, and law school aspirants to avail themselves of the most relevant skills to thrive in an increasingly competitive job market. We would like to express our gratitude to the institution for acquainting us with like-minded peers and setting in motion our vision of Dreadnought Robotics.
