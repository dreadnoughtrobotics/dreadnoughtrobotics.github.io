Turret • Robosumo

Robo Sumo is an event where two robots wrestle to push each other out of a defined ring. They are divided into different weight categories; points are provided based on the set of rules and regulations. Our bot is built to compete using squared DC geared motors of 300 rpm to provide high torque and SmartDriveDue -30(MDDS30) to provide great steadiness which uses proportional control system. Due to the fierce nature of the competition, hard rubber wheels are used. The selection of all the components that go into the bot has an effect on how the exoskeleton is completed using mild steel, wood and aluminium. Keeping safety in mind.






Skyline • Drone

Quadcopters are a category in drones propelled by four rotors, built to compete in racing, task completion, and combat contests. Our drone is controlled by an EACHINE GPS F4 flight controller with integrated OSD, betaflight compatible, and capable of autonomous flight. 40A ESCs are incorporated which distributes power from 4S battery to the high rpm motors mounted with the propellers on a sturdy carbon fiber frame to provide a stable flight. FPV cameras are utilized with VR headsets for optimum piloting performance.






Kaiser • Nitro Car

Nitro cars have engines powered with fuel doped by nitromethane which gives enhanced performance than the electric car in terms of speed and run-time. Built on durable aluminium chassis, our nitro car is equipped with powerful Nitrostar F3. 5 HPI engine controlled by 2.4GHz radio system along with aluminium heat sink, braces, shock towers, etc. with a pull starter system, all combined to provide faster acceleration and higher speeds specially made for racing competitions.






Torpedo • All Terrain Vehicle

ATV robot is designed and built to deal with different terrains than conventional vehicles. They have to complete the arena by crossing the given obstacles in the shortest time possible. We use drift tire buggy wheels to increase grip. Cytron SmartDriveDue -30(MDDS30) provides good stability for current flow with 300RPM Johnson Dc A-class geared motors providing high torque, the exoskeleton used is mild steel, aluminium alloy. This allows us to control technical aspects of racing where each bot made depends on the tarmac.






Mayhem • Drag Race

RC robots specially designed for racing with enhanced performance on speed are used for drag racing. In this race, bots compete against each other on a straight track to reach the end. Points are awarded based on the shortest time required to complete the track and penalties are provided according to a specific set of rules.
